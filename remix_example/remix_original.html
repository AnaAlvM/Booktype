You can remix your own manuals. Select a manual (or manuals) in Step one with the drop down menu and drag chapters to the box on the right. Choose an output format (html/pdf) in step two and tweak the style. In step three you can export the remix and also use the (beta) ajax provided to put a live manual in your web page.
